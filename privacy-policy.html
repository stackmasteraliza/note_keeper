Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

Note Keeper is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All your notes stay on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Note Keeper operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device. This includes:

  • Note titles and content
  • Note colors and preferences
  • Creation timestamps

None of this information ever leaves your device.

Data Storage

All your notes are stored locally on your device's internal storage. We do not use any cloud services or external servers to store your data.

If you uninstall the app, all your notes will be permanently deleted from your device.

Third-Party Services

Note Keeper does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. Your data remains completely private.

Permissions

Note Keeper requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your notes locally on your device

We only request permissions that are essential for the app's core functionality.

Children's Privacy

Note Keeper does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
